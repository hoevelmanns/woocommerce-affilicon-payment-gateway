Connect your Woocommerce shop to the payment platform AffiliCon.

== Description ==

With the AffiliCon Woocommerce Gateway you can extend Woocommerce with the comfortable AffiliCon-Checkout. Your customers simply choose AffiliCon as their payment method in the checkout page and you will be immediately taken to our checkout form. AffiliCon will then take over the subsequent processes for you and transmit the status of your orders fully automatically to Woocommerce.
